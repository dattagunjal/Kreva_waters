Our 6-Step Purification Process

1. Sand Filter

Removes suspended particles, dirt, and physical impurities from raw water.

2. Carbon Filter

Eliminates chlorine, unpleasant taste, odor, and organic compounds.

3. Reverse Osmosis

Filters out dissolved solids, salts, heavy metals, and micro-particles.

4. Micron Filters

Catches remaining microscopic particles through precision cartridges.

5. UV Sterilization

Uses ultraviolet rays to neutralize biological impurities and bacteria.

6. Ozonation

Oxygenation and final sealing stage to retain freshness for a long time.
